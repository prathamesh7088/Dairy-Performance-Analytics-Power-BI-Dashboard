# Dairy-Performance-Analytics-Power-BI-Dashboard

An end-to-end Power BI analytics solution designed to analyze the complete dairy business workflow, from milk procurement by farmers to milk sales to customers.

This dashboard helps identify milk production trends, farmer contributions, milk quality, revenue generation, and customer purchasing behavior.

🔍 Key Insights

Milk collection trends by month and shift

Cow vs Buffalo milk production comparison

Top performing farmers and customers

Milk sales analysis and payment mode tracking

Revenue analysis for better business decision-making

Power BI

DAX

Data Modeling

Data Visualization

📈 Business Value

Identifies seasonal milk trends

Improves pricing and incentive strategies

Helps recognize top farmers and customers

Supports data-driven decisions for dairy businesses
